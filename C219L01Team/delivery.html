Delivery

1. Little Toy Kingdom ONLY delivers to locations within Singapore.

2. Purchases above $120 will enjoy free delivery in Singapore.

3. $5 delivery fee applies to all other locations within Singapore, regardless of weight & size of items.

4. $5 admin fee will be imposed on orders with overseas delivery locations requesting for refunds.

5. Items will be delivered within 2 to 4 working days (excluding weekends and holidays) after full payment.

6. Qxpress will be handling the delivery of your purchases.
